Government Scholarship

Start your journey with us. Apply for our diverse range of programs.

Our admissions process is straightforward and designed to assist you in joining the Sherubtse College community. Here are some key points:

  • Application Deadline: July 15th, 2024
  • Eligibility Criteria: Applicants must have passed Class XII with a minimum of 55% in subjects relevant to their program. For example, to be eligible for the BSc in Physics program, applicants must have a minimum of 55% in Physics, 45% in Mathematics, and Chemistry.
  • Required Documents: Citizenship Identity Card, Transfer Certificate, Class 10 Mark Sheet and Pass Certificate, Class 12 Mark Sheet and Pass Certificate

Leadership Scholarship

This scholarship is available for applicants to the BSc in Life Science, BSc in Mathematics, BSc in Chemistry, and BSc in Physics programmes. To qualify, applicants must:

  • Be a regular student in 2023
  • Have served as School Captain in the academic year 2023
  • Meet the minimum criteria for the selected programme at Sherubtse College
  • Merit ranking based on aggregate scores in English and three best subjects

Apply via email to records.sherubtse@rub.edu.bt by 19th April 2024. The application form is available on www.sherubtse.edu.bt.

King's Scholarship

The King's Scholarship aims to support talented students in pursuing higher education. It is awarded based on academic excellence and leadership qualities demonstrated throughout high school.

Eligibility Criteria:

  • Top academic performance in secondary education
  • Proven leadership roles or community service
  • Meeting minimum admission requirements for the programme

Application details will be announced on the Royal University of Bhutan's website. Visit www.rub.edu.bt for updates.

Self-Funding Option

Students who do not qualify for scholarships can opt for the self-funding route. This allows students to pursue their desired programme by covering tuition fees independently.

Annual Tuition Fees:

  • Arts and Humanities Programmes: Nu. 75,457
  • Science and Engineering Programmes: Nu. 93,479

Payments can be made in two installments, with 50% due at the beginning of each semester. An annual increase of 2% applies to all tuition fees.

For more details, contact the Admissions Office at admissions.sherubtse@rub.edu.bt.
