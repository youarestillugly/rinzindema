Admissions

Start your journey with us. Apply for our diverse range of programs.

Our admissions process is straightforward and designed to assist you in joining the Sherubtse College community. Here are some key points:

  • Application Deadline: July 15th, 2024
  • Eligibility Criteria: Applicants must have passed Class XII with a minimum of 55% in the subjects relevant to their program. For example, to be eligible for the BSc in Physics program, applicants must have a minimum of 55% in Physics, 45% in Mathematics and Chemistry.
  • Required Documents: Citizenship Identity Card and Transfer Certificate. Class 10 Mark Sheet and Pass Certificate. Class 12 Mark Sheet and Pass Certificate
