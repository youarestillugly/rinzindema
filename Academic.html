Admission into RUB Programmes for the Academic Year 2024

The online application for College of Science and Technology, Gyalpozhing College of Information Technology, Paro College of Education, Gedu College of Business Studies, Samtse College of Education, and three new programmes at Sherubtse College will open on 1st March and close on 22nd March 2024. The online application links will be announced by the Royal University of Bhutan.

Applications for the College of Natural Resources, Jigme Namgyel Engineering College, and Sherubtse College (BSc in Physics, BSc in Chemistry, BSc in Mathematics, and BSc in Life Science) will open on 5th April and close on 5th May 2024.

Tuition Fees for Academic Year 2024-2025

For Arts and Humanities programmes, tuition is set at Nu. 75,457, while for Science and Engineering programmes, it is Nu. 93,479. An annual 2% increase applies. Tuition is payable in two installments, with 50% due at the start of each semester.

For additional details, please visit the respective colleges’ websites.

Special Scholarship in Leadership for 6 Students

This scholarship is available for applicants to the BSc in Life Science, BSc in Mathematics, BSc in Chemistry, and BSc in Physics programmes. Aspiring candidates must fulfill the following criteria:

  • Be a regular student in 2023
  • Have served as the School Captain in the academic year 2023
  • Meet the minimum criteria for the programme at Sherubtse College
  • Merit ranking will be based on the aggregate of four subjects (English plus three best subjects)

To apply, send an email to records.sherubtse@rub.edu.bt by 19th April 2024. The application form can be downloaded from www.sherubtse.edu.bt. If no acknowledgment is received by 23rd April 2024, please contact the Student Record Office at 04 535341.
