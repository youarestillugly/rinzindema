Vision

An internationally recognized institute in Liberal Arts and Sciences with an emphasis on GNH value-based learning.

Mission

To provide GNH inspired quality tertiary education in Liberal Arts and Sciences. To contribute to the development of knowledge-based society through knowledge creation, sharing, and advocacy.

Core Values

The core values that guide us at Sherubtse College are:

  • Professionalism: Upholding the highest standards in all our endeavors.
  • Innovation: Encouraging creativity and new ideas to drive progress.
  • Passion for Learning: A deep commitment to continuous growth and development.
  • Sense of Ownership: Taking responsibility for our actions and outcomes.
  • Community Services: Engaging with and giving back to our community.
  • Academic Excellence: Striving to maintain the highest standards in education.
