Vision

To promote an on-campus culture of wellness that ensures happiness, psychological well-being & lifelong success.

Mission

To create and provide an environment that fosters students’ growth, development, and psychological well-being through counselling services & awareness programs.

Background Information

The College has a counselling service center called the Happiness and Well-Being Center, run by well-trained Co-counsellors. It is located within the Student Service Village. It was established in February, 2016 to cater to the counselling services for students and staff of the college.

Services

The center offers a variety of services designed to assist students and staff in maintaining their well-being and mental health. These services include individual counselling, group therapy, stress management workshops, and wellness programs.
