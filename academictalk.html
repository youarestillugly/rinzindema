Academic Talk

By Sherubtse / December 6, 2023

Two Professors from the Department of Mathematical Sciences delivered an academic talk as part of the Monday morning assembly activity. The talk was attended by the faculties and students of the first and third years.

Professor Yu-Ju Kuo spoke on Digital World: Identity and Opportunities, and Professor Mark A. Roosa spoke on The Art of Giving and Receiving Advice.

The Professors from the US are attached to Sherubtse as Fulbright Scholars for 10 months with assistance from RCSC. The Professors are teaching Mathematics, Statistics, and Data Science.
