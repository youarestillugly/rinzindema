Examination Records

Please take note of the following information for obtaining duplicate student records documents from Sherubtse College:

  • There is no charge for the first issuance of mark sheets and certificates, including the provisional certificate. Provisional certificates will only be issued prior to the graduates’ convocation.
  • Duplicate certificates will be issued by the Registrar.
  • Duplicate transcripts will only be processed after providing evidence of payment.
  • Payment should be made to Sherubtse College, Account Number [Insert Account Number Here].
  • The evidence of payment and relevant details should be emailed to records.sherubtse@rub.edu.bt.
  • The necessary documents should be collected in person by a designated representative from the college.

Mailing Official Transcripts

  • An electronic copy of the transcripts will be sent by the college at no cost.
  • For hard copies of the transcripts, duplicate transcript fees and postage charges apply for mailing.
  • Please send your request to records.sherubtse@rub.edu.bt along with a scanned copy of your transcript.
  • Processing time is 3-5 working days, and it may extend further during vacation periods. Please consider this timeframe when making plans.
