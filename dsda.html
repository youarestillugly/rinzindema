Bachelors of Data Science & Data Analytics

Programme Overview

As the global economy experiences unprecedented disruption due to emerging technological advancements, an increasing emphasis is being placed on the importance of graduates gaining both transferable skills and specific qualifications. This ensures better long-term employment outcomes in an ever-changing global workplace.

The Bachelors of Data Science and Data Analytics programme is designed to produce students with adaptive mindsets to handle job disruptions and seize various opportunities. The programme will emphasize producing highly skilled and knowledgeable graduates, ready for the challenges of the future.

The programme focuses on data handling, visualization, and curation through modules in Data Science, Statistics, and Mathematics. Students will learn to apply statistical and computational principles to solve real-world problems with large-scale data. Graduates will be equipped to turn chaotic data into actionable insights, helping leaders make better strategic decisions.

Data experiences will play a central role in all modules, from introductory to advanced capstone or research projects. Graduates will be able to perform data wrangling, analyse raw data from various sources, and generate powerful insights for informed decision-making.
