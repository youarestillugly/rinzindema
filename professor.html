Professor Wim Highlights the Importance of Mental Health in Young People

By Sherubtse / December 6, 2023

Mr. Wim, a faculty member of Social Science at Sherubtse College, delivered a talk on the importance of mental health among young people. With the semester-end examinations approaching, many students struggle to cope with external stressors such as time management, irregular eating habits, and disorganization. Beyond these external factors, mental health issues are often overlooked, leading many young people to suffer silently.

Professor Wim highlighted that anxiety disorders are prevalent among youth, causing intense fear, worry, or anxiety, which can interfere with daily activities and quality of life.

He shared three key insights to help students address mental health challenges:

  • Stay True to Yourself: Drawing from his own college experiences, Professor Wim advised students to motivate themselves and connect with their inner purpose. Reflecting on why you are at Sherubtse and finding meaning in your actions can help alleviate mental stress.
  • Build a Support Network: Confiding in trusted individuals and engaging with friends can provide mutual support and encouragement. He emphasized the importance of collaboration, such as studying together, discussing, and preparing for exams as a team, which benefits everyone involved.
  • Plan and Organize: To minimize stress, students should organize their tasks, plan ahead, and avoid procrastination, as last-minute efforts often increase anxiety.
